    📖 Quick Reference – Newton's Three Laws
    1st Law (Inertia): An object at rest stays at rest, and an object in motion stays in motion unless acted upon by a net force.
    2nd Law: F = ma — The acceleration of an object depends on its mass and the net force applied.
    3rd Law: For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.
